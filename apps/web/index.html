Area Code

See what's alive in your city.

Area Code is a real-time venue discovery and rewards platform for South Africa. Open the app to see a live map of cafes, restaurants, bars, and nightlife venues in Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban — with each spot showing whether it's quiet, active, buzzing, or popping right now.

Check in when you arrive to earn rewards from the venue, climb local leaderboards, build streaks, and discover places that match your taste in coffee, food, and music.

Sign in with Google or with an email and password. There is no phone number required. Your location is used only to verify check-ins at the moment they happen and is never stored.

For consumers

  • Live map of South African venues, updated in real time as people check in
  • Rewards from local cafes, bars, restaurants, and nightlife venues
  • Loyalty tiers, streaks, leaderboards, and a friends feed
  • Music taste profiles powered by Spotify and Apple Music (optional)

For businesses

Area Code gives venue owners a live dashboard, anonymized customer intelligence reports, and a rewards engine. Visit business.areacode.co.za to learn more.
