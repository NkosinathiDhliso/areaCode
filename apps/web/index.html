Area Code

A real-time venue discovery and rewards app for South Africa.

About Area Code

Area Code is a mobile and web application that lets people in Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban see which cafes, restaurants, bars, and nightlife venues are alive right now. Each venue shows a live status (quiet, active, buzzing, or popping) updated in real time as customers check in.

Customers check in when they arrive at a participating venue to earn rewards from that venue, climb local leaderboards, build streaks, and discover places that match their taste in coffee, food, and music.

You can sign in to Area Code with Google or with an email and password. There is no phone number required. Your location is used only to verify check-ins at the moment they happen and is never stored.

For consumers

  • Live map of South African venues, updated in real time as people check in
  • Rewards from local cafes, bars, restaurants, and nightlife venues
  • Loyalty tiers, streaks, leaderboards, and a friends feed
  • Music taste profiles powered by Spotify and Apple Music (optional)

For businesses

Area Code gives venue owners a live dashboard, anonymized customer intelligence reports, and a rewards engine. Visit https://business.areacode.co.za/ to learn more.
